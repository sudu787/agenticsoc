# SecureFlow AI — Demo Guide

## 5-Step Judge Demo (3 minutes)

### Step 1 — War Room (0:00–0:30)
**Navigate to:** http://localhost:3000
**Show:** Live alert ticker, 5 agent activity feed, threat gauge
**Say:** "This is our Security War Room — 5 AI agents are permanently online"

### Step 2 — Launch Attack (0:30–1:00)
**Navigate to:** http://localhost:3000/demo  
**Click:** 🚨 Launch Ransomware Attack
**Watch:** Kill chain timeline animating through 9 stages
**Say:** "I've triggered an APT29 ransomware campaign — watch the AI respond"

### Step 3 — Knowledge Graph (1:00–1:45)
**Navigate to:** http://localhost:3000/graph
**Click:** Any compromised node → Propagate Risk
**Show:** Risk cascade animation across 14 assets
**Say:** "The graph maps every relationship and computes the blast radius in milliseconds"

### Step 4 — Autonomous Response + XAI (1:45–2:20)
**Navigate to:** http://localhost:3000/autonomous
**Click:** "Why this action?" on any pending item
**Show:** Evidence chain with graph + memory + RAG citations
**Say:** "Every AI decision shows its reasoning before asking for approval"

### Step 5 — Executive Dashboard (2:20–3:00)
**Navigate to:** http://localhost:3000/executive
**Click:** Generate CISO Report
**Show:** Full modal with risk, compliance, forecast, recommendations
**Say:** "The CISO gets a boardroom briefing in one click — no report writing"
